Privacy Policy Last updated: June 24, 2026 1. Information We Collect The Shorts Automation App ("the App") collects and processes the following information: - API credentials and access tokens for connected platforms (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, OpenAI, Pexels) - Video content metadata including titles, descriptions, and hashtags - Content scheduling preferences and posting logs - Basic analytics returned by connected platforms (e.g., post status) 2. How We Use Your Information Your information is used exclusively to: - Authenticate with connected platforms on your behalf - Publish scheduled video content as configured - Log posting results for your review - Provide error notifications and troubleshooting 3. Data Storage - API credentials are stored encrypted in the n8n credential vault - Posted content is stored in your connected cloud storage bucket (AWS S3 or Cloudflare R2) - Posting logs are stored in your connected Google Sheet - No data is shared with third parties 4. Third-Party Services The App integrates with: - OpenAI (script generation, voiceover synthesis) - Pexels (background video sourcing) - YouTube (video publishing via YouTube Data API v3) - TikTok (video publishing via TikTok Content Posting API) - Instagram (video publishing via Meta Graph API) - Facebook (video publishing via Meta Graph API) - Google Sheets (logging) Each service has its own privacy policy that governs data submitted to it. 5. Data Retention - Posted videos remain in your cloud storage until you delete them - Posting logs remain in your Google Sheet until you delete them - API credentials are retained until you revoke them or delete the n8n credential 6. Your Rights You can: - Revoke platform access at any time by disconnecting credentials in n8n - Delete posted content directly on each platform - Delete your Google Sheet log at any time - Request deletion of all App data by deleting the n8n workflow 7. Data Security We use HTTPS encryption for all API communication. Credentials are stored in n8n's encrypted credential vault. We do not store payment information. 8. Children's Privacy The App is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. 9. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
